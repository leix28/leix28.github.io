About Me

I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science from MIT in 2023, advised by Dr. Kalyan Veeramachaneni. My current research interests lie in robustness in natural language processing, in particular large language models, text generation and model robustness. During the past few years, I worked on several interesting topics including synthetic data generation, generative adversarial networks, sequence-to-sequence text summarization, word embeddings and deep reinforcement learning. I got a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in June 2017. I worked with Zhiyuan Liu(THU) and J. Zico Kolter(CMU) during my undergrad.
